Why Choose the Frame Drum Sample Instrument?

Two Versions to Suit Your Needs

The Frame Drum Sample Instrument comes in two carefully crafted versions to cater to different creative requirements:

FREE Version

Immerse yourself in the essence of the Frame Drum with a collection of high-quality audio loops that showcase its remarkable sound.

PRO Version

Unlock the full potential of the Frame Drum with features designed for unparalleled realism and flexibility:

  • 8 Round Robin Samples: Enjoy an organic and lifelike playing experience, capturing subtle tonal variations.
  • Two Instruments:
    • Solo: For a focused and intimate sound.
    • Duo: Panned left and right to create a wide and immersive stereo effect.

High Quality Recording

Every note of the Frame Drum Sample Instrument has been captured with exceptional clarity. We used a combination of Zoom H4 and Lewitt LCT 040 microphones to ensure the highest audio fidelity, allowing the true character of the instrument to shine through.

Exclusive PRO Features

As a PRO user, you gain access to exclusive resources that take your compositions to the next level:

  • MIDI Files: A treasure trove of ready-to-use patterns to kickstart your creativity.
  • Rhythmic Foundations: Explore the traditional Arabic/Turkish rhythm known as “Duek” alongside newly added variant rhythms. These rhythmic templates provide a solid foundation for your compositions while sparking new ideas.

Endless Creative Possibilities

From delicate, intimate passages to bold, cinematic textures, the Frame Drum Sample Instrument adapts to your vision. Its versatility makes it a must-have for composers and producers seeking a sound that’s both authentic and captivating.
